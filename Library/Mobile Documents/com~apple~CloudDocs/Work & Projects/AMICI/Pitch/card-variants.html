Card Design Variants

Five design options for the "Why Now" cards. Pick one and I'll apply it to v1.2.

Variant A — Icon accent

Replaces the faded number with a themed icon in a soft-accent box. Adds a visual anchor to each card without relying on numbering.

📈

Corporate events are booming

Post-pandemic, companies are investing heavily in in-person team building, client entertainment, and culture events. Sweden's event market is growing at 9.1% CAGR, the fastest in Europe.

✂️

Companies are cutting agency spend

Budgets are tighter. Companies want the quality of a professionally organized event without the 15–20% agency markup. They need a self-serve tool, not a middleman.

⚡

AI lets small teams move fast

AI development tools let a four-person team build what used to take twenty. We ship features in days, not months.
